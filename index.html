Introduction

Emotion recognition is a rapidly evolving field within facial recognition technology. By analyzing facial expressions, we can gain deeper insights into human emotions and psychological states. This project focuses on developing a system capable of identifying and classifying emotions from images and videos.

The potential applications of this technology include:

  • Healthcare: Monitoring and detecting psychological issues through patient emotions.
  • Marketing: Analyzing customer reactions to products and services.
  • Education: Tracking students' emotions to enhance teaching methods.
  • Entertainment: Creating richer user experiences through emotion detection.

Technology Behind It

The emotion recognition system employs cutting-edge technologies such as:

  • Machine Learning: Deep learning models enable accurate emotion classification based on facial features.
  • Face Detection: Identifies and locates faces within images.
  • Emotion Analysis: Uses algorithms to analyze expressions and determine corresponding emotions.
